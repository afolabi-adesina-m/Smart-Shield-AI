Highway risk you can read before you drive.

Smart-Shield fuses Ontario 511-style alerts (NLP), road-surface vision, and environmental collision risk into one Safety Score S — with speed guidance for Highway 401 and other 400-series corridors.

Three-brain architecture

Each pillar outputs a 0–1 risk signal. Fusion weights produce the operational Safety Score.

Pillar T

NLP Brain

TF-IDF over hazard lexicon from unstructured alerts (ice, collision, closure) → text risk T.

Pillar V

Vision Brain

ResNet18 (+ optional autoencoder) on road imagery → surface hazard confidence V.

Pillar E

Environment

Weather / surface / lighting signals calibrated to literature → environmental index E.

Interactive Safety Score

Drag the sliders — S, risk band, and speed advice update live. Formula: S = (wT·T + wV·V + wE·E) × 100
